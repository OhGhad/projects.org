OhGhad! Akwaaba

(Ghana Amusement Development)

Ohghad is an amusement development program that seeks to liaise with chiefs and kings or local assemblies to apportion parcels of land intended for development projects only for our number of proposed projects. This is a new initiative to raise funds for land and land development projects that consist of bringing more amusement to Africa starting in Ghana. We recognize the value of chieftaincy institutions as a pillar of development.

Considering some of the root issues that in the past were deal breakers for such developments like reliable and strong power, water sources, and of course quality internet resources. Today, access to off-grid power solutions are more available and affordable during the development planning phase.

Ghana is honored to welcome such developments to accompany the Famous historical Slave Dungeons and Last Bath Rivers!

Join us in influencing how entertainment will develop in Ghana; by working together, we can achieve great things!

By contributing to our crowdfunding campaign, you join the OhGhad family and help us develop a lively, family-friendly destination that honors Ghana's rich culture and spirit.
